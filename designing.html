Overview

At Truevosry Academy, our comprehensive design course is designed to provide you with a solid foundation in fundamental design principles and techniques. You will acquire the skills necessary to create visually captivating designs across various mediums.

Throughout the course, you will explore the digital realm of design, gaining hands-on experience with industry-standard software such as Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. This will enable you to unleash your creativity and effectively bring your design ideas to life.

The curriculum also includes an in-depth study of important aspects of design, such as composition, color theory, typography, and layout. You will learn how to combine these elements harmoniously to create aesthetically pleasing and impactful designs. In addition, the course covers the field of user experience (UX) design, a vital aspect of modern design practice. You will delve into UX principles and techniques, discovering how to create intuitive interfaces and conduct user research to optimize user satisfaction.

To further enhance your learning experience, the course incorporates engaging projects and assignments. Through these hands-on activities, you will have the opportunity to apply your skills and creativity to real-world design challenges. From designing logos and branding materials to crafting user-friendly websites and mobile applications, you will develop a diverse portfolio that showcases your abilities.

By completing our design course, you will be well-prepared to pursue exciting career opportunities in the design industry. Whether you aspire to work as a graphic designer, UX designer, web designer, or other design-related roles, our comprehensive curriculum equips you with the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed.

Join Truevosry Academy's design course today and embark on a journey of creativity, skill development, and professional growth in the dynamic world of design.

What you'll learn

At Truevosry Academy, we offer a diverse range of designing courses that cater to various creative interests. Our programs include:

  • Diploma in Interior Design: This course focuses on developing skills and knowledge in interior design, covering topics such as space planning, color schemes, furniture selection, and creating aesthetically pleasing and functional interiors.
  • Diploma in Fashion Design: This program provides comprehensive training in fashion design, teaching you the art of creating clothing and accessories. You will learn about fashion illustration, garment construction, fabric selection, and trends in the fashion industry.
  • Diploma in Animation & VFX: This course delves into the world of animation and visual effects, equipping you with the technical skills needed to bring characters and visual elements to life. You will learn animation principles, digital modeling, texturing, lighting, and compositing techniques.
  • Diploma in Graphic Design: This program focuses on developing skills in graphic design, covering areas such as layout design, typography, color theory, and image editing. You will learn how to create visually appealing designs for various mediums, including print and digital platforms.
  • Diploma in Web Designing: This course specializes in web design, teaching you how to create engaging and user-friendly websites. You will learn about web development languages, responsive design principles, user interface (UI) design, and website optimization techniques.

By enrolling in our designing courses, you will gain practical skills and industry knowledge that are essential for pursuing a successful career in the respective fields. Our experienced instructors and hands-on learning approach will help you develop a strong portfolio that showcases your creativity and expertise.

Whether you aspire to become an interior designer, fashion designer, animator, graphic designer, or web designer, Truevosry Academy provides the necessary training and support to help you achieve your goals. Join us today and unleash your creative potential in the world of design.
