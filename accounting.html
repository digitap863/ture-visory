What you'll learn

At Truevosry Academy, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that equips you with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in the accounting field. Our courses focus on a range of topics, including:

  • Masters in Global Accounting: This program provides in-depth knowledge and expertise in global accounting practices, including international financial reporting standards, cross-border transactions, and the complexities of multinational financial statements.
  • Post Graduate Diploma in Indian & Foreign Accounting: This diploma program covers both Indian and foreign accounting principles, enabling you to understand and apply accounting standards and practices in diverse business environments.
  • Diploma in Indian & Foreign Accounting: This diploma program focuses on developing a strong foundation in Indian and foreign accounting principles, preparing you for entry-level positions in accounting and finance.
  • Diploma in Corporate Accounts and Taxation: This diploma program specializes in corporate accounting and taxation, equipping you with the skills needed to handle financial statements, tax compliance, and regulatory requirements for companies.
  • Advanced Diploma in Financial Management: This advanced program focuses on financial management principles, teaching you how to analyze financial data, make informed decisions, and effectively manage resources within an organization.
  • SAP FICO: This course provides hands-on training in SAP FICO (Financial Accounting and Controlling), one of the most widely used enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems. You will learn to manage financial transactions, generate financial reports, and gain expertise in SAP FICO modules.
  • SAP MM: This course offers comprehensive training in SAP MM (Materials Management), a key module in SAP ERP. You will learn how to manage procurement processes, inventory management, and vendor relationships using SAP MM.
  • Advanced MS Excel: This course focuses on enhancing your skills in Microsoft Excel, a powerful tool for data analysis and financial modeling. You will learn advanced functions, formulas, and data manipulation techniques to streamline accounting processes.
  • Tally with GST / GCC VAT: This course combines training in Tally, a popular accounting software, with an in-depth understanding of Goods and Services Tax (GST) in India or Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) Value Added Tax (VAT) regulations. You will gain practical skills in using Tally for accurate financial recording and compliance with tax regulations.
