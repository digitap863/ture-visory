Enhance Your Career with a Diploma in Logistics and Supply Chain Management
Discover the exciting world of logistics and supply chain management through our comprehensive Diploma program. Gain the essential skills and knowledge needed to succeed in this rapidly growing industry. With a focus on practical training and industry-relevant topics, our program prepares you for a successful career in logistics and supply chain management.

Key Program Highlights:

At Truevosry Academy, our comprehensive curriculum ensures that you gain a thorough understanding of supply chain management. Through our courses, you will learn:

  • Logistics: Learn the fundamental principles of logistics, including transportation, inventory management, and warehouse operations.
  • Warehouse and Inventory Management: Develop the skills necessary to efficiently manage warehouse operations and optimize inventory levels.
  • Supply Chain Coordination: This module focuses on the coordination and collaboration among supply chain partners to improve overall performance. You will learn about strategies for managing relationships, information sharing, and aligning goals across the supply chain.
  • Supply Chain Management: Understand the intricacies of supply chain management, from procurement to distribution, and learn how to streamline processes for maximum efficiency.
  • Export-Import Trade and Documentation: Master the complexities of international trade, including export-import procedures and documentation requirements.
  • International Marketing Management: Gain insights into global marketing strategies and learn how to adapt marketing techniques to international markets. International Business Management: Explore the intricacies of conducting business on a global scale, including cross-cultural management and international business strategies.
  • Shipping and Port Management: Acquire a deep understanding of shipping operations, port management, and the regulatory framework governing international shipping.
  • Shipping and Port Management: Acquire a deep understanding of shipping operations, port management, and the regulatory framework governing international shipping.

Upon successful completion of the program, receive a prestigious government certification that validates your expertise in logistics and supply chain management.
